Ingrediants 📃

Dry Ingrediants 🧂

  • 1 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingrediants

  • 1 Cup Unsalted Butter,room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Mix- ins🍫

  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips
  • optional: 1/2 chopped walnuts or pecanes

    • Instruction 🍽

    1. Preheat your oven to 3500F(175 0C).Line a baking sheet with parement paper or a silicone mat 🔥
    2. In a small bowl,whisk together the flour and baking soda:set aside🧹
    3. In a large bowl,beat the butter with both sugar until creamy and light and texture.🍬🧹
